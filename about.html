Transforming businesses with digital commerce

At Amazon, we are on a mission to digitize over one crore small and medium businesses in India by 2025. And with that in mind, we launched a new initiative to digitally transform small businesses across India — Smart Commerce by Amazon.

Our vision

To transform the way businesses operate in India by enabling them to digitally start, manage, and exponentially scale to new heights, irrespective of where they are — in their own stores, on Amazon, and everywhere in between.

Open a world of possibilities with Smart Commerce by Amazon

Smart Commerce by Amazon is a suite of SaaS-based solutions that enable businesses to reach new customers online, streamline and simplify operations, and reduce costs. With Smart Commerce, small businesses can digitize their offline operations, provide enhanced in-store shopping experience to their walk-in customers, and create their own online stores to serve customers directly, and more.

Stores of any size can now take advantage of Amazon’s shopping innovations, logistics, digital payments, and more to provide a reliable and trustworthy experience to their customers — no matter where they are — in their physical store, directly through their own online store, or on Amazon.in.

Our solutions

SmartPOS by Amazon enables store owners to digitize their own store operations such as billing, staff management, inventory and catalog management, reporting, and more. Businesses can now better engage their customers by rolling out discounts and promotions like never before, thereby creating a seamless in-store shopping experience.
