How to set up your SmartBiz store?

Creating your own eCommerce store with SmartBiz allows your business to go from local to global in minutes. Unlike developing an eCommerce website from scratch, the SmartBiz app provides amazing features that lets you sell the way you want to your customers online.

With SmartBiz, you can now create an online store for free within minutes, without any complex coding or technical knowledge.

Here are three steps to register your account with SmartBiz by Amazon:

1. Download app online:

To begin using SmartBiz, all you need to do is download the app. This is currently invite-only, so join our interest list by clicking here. We will reach out to you and get you started on your free trial.

2. Sign up on app:

  1. Create an account for your business
  2. Enter your name, mobile number, e-mail ID (optional), and password
  3. Confirm your mobile number by entering the received OTP

3. Fill in the necessary business details:

  1. Enter your store name and category(s*). Be careful while updating your store name as it is non-editable
  2. Set the location of your business and mention the complete address, including street, landmark, pin code, and state. You can also select the location on the map. You must ensure this is accurate to estimate the delivery radius and so that customers can view this for picking up orders. This can be edited later if required
