Key Features

The last two years have been unprecedented, as the pandemic has transformed how we live, communicate with each other, and do our business. One of the most significant changes we have noticed is how consumer behavior has evolved gradually. As e-commerce is on the rise, customers browse products, make purchases, and conduct digital payments — all online.

At the same time, e-commerce is offering businesses the opportunity to cater to the needs of their customers, minimize the impact of stalled sales, revive businesses, and prevent the loss of jobs or even create new jobs in the process.

Accelerated by the government’s push towards building a ‘Digital India’ and ‘Make in India,’ lakhs of MSMEs have started embracing digital technologies to start, build, and scale their businesses. E-commerce has helped to create a level playing field for businesses of all sizes and scales. Anyone from a single entrepreneur to the largest business can now effectively break down geographical barriers and run a national or even a global business with limited investments.

SmartBiz by Amazon is a new initiative to enable small businesses across India to easily start/digitize their businesses, enabling them to reach more customers, simplify their online operations, and reduce costs. It will help sellers build digital foundations easily for their businesses. Our easy-to-use solution makes it very simple for small businesses to set up their own online stores and sell their products online, offer multiple online payment options to their customers, and fulfill their online orders easily, quickly, and effectively. Sellers can now use Amazon’s tools and technologies to sell directly to their customers without requiring any presence even on the Amazon.in marketplace.

Some key features of SmartBiz are:

Entrepreneurs can set up their online business and create their own eCommerce website in a matter of minutes without requiring complex technical coding.

Access your business from just anywhere with easy-to-use features through the SmartBiz mobile app.

List unlimited products on your online store in simple steps. You can choose from various categories and sell multiple products easily.

Entrepreneurs and small businesses can sell their products online even if they don’t have a GST number*, provided they do not cross the turnover threshold limit as stipulated under the GST law.

Detailed training material, step-by-step instruction guides, and video manuals are available to help you understand how to get started online.

You can offer multiple online payment options to your customers to purchase your products, all through a single app. From UPI to bank transactions, there are plenty of options to set up your payments.

Sellers can sell their products on their own terms for payments, returns, and refunds. They can also enjoy a two-day payment settlement.

You can showcase all your products in one place and promote your online store with a QR code or store link on social media and chat commerce applications. This way, you can redirect your customers to your own online store across different channels, thereby consolidating and managing orders from a single place.

Reliable call support centers and multiple resources available. You can also connect with us on Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube.

Easy and hassle-free shipping with reliable shipping partners and quick delivery service.

Pay zero fees for our online store builder. Free to use for the next three months as an introductory offer.

Created by Amazon, which is trusted by over 11 lakh+ sellers all over the country for its quality services.

*Without GST (subject to the turnover threshold and compulsory registration requirements stipulated under the GST law), sellers can only sell within state borders. For inter-state or national shipping, by law, you still need to update the GST registration number. We will share an update shortly on how to go about this process.

The SmartBiz app is designed to help you easily set up an end-to-end online store and start scaling your digital presence among customers from all over India.
