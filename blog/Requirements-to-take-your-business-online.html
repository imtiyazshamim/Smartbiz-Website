Requirements to set up your online store

It is no secret that in today’s day and age when it comes to purchasing products, customers prefer going online. According to a study by Think with Google, 69% of customers’ purchase journey begins online. Customers find that shopping online has multiple benefits, such as convenience, a variety of product options in one place, better deals and prices, easy product returns and replacements, the ability to view real reviews and ratings, and much more. As a result, exclusively traditional companies are also gradually going online. This was further accelerated by the evolving customer behavior during the pandemic when buying and selling online presented a relatively safer way of commerce in line with social distancing protocols.

Are you still thinking about taking your business online?

You might be hesitant to sell your products online due to lack of time and money required to make your website. You may think it involves too many complications, technicalities, and creative thought processes that could be confusing.

Fret not! That's where SmartBiz by Amazon comes into the picture.

SmartBiz is a website builder that can help you create your own e-commerce store completely integrated with shipping service providers and online payment services within minutes. Just follow a few simple steps to set it up, and you’re ready to sell!

Requirements to set up your online store

  1. Download the SmartBiz app* on your phone and create an account to register yourself.
  2. List your products online and upload your catalog information.
  3. Set up a payment gateway to accept payments online from customers.
  4. Plan shipping and delivery for your products either yourself using your own courier partners or by using the shipping service providers on the SmartBiz app.
