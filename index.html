You can start selling your products online on your own website created using SmartBiz without any GST. However, in order to enable inter-state shipping, you will need GST. Therefore, although optional for selling within the state, it is recommended to get a GST number in order to grow your business online and reach customers across India.

Please note that in the trial phase, we do not require GST details from sellers and therefore, we are limiting deliveries only within the sellers’ respective state boundaries. However, we will soon update this and allow inter-state shipping. Watch this space for further updates.
